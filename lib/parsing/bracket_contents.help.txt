in text
-------

[page name] -> hyperlink to the page, hyperlink text is the page name

[url] -> external hyperlink, hyperlink text is the url
[url trailing text] -> eternal hyperlink, hyperlink text is the trailing test

not in txt
----------

<img>
[urlforimage] show image at default size
[urlforimage width] show image on page at specified width reserving aspect ratio
