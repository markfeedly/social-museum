in text
-------

[page name] -> hyperlink to the page, hyperlink text is the page name

[url] -> external hyperlink, hyperlink text is the url
[url trailing text] -> eternal hyperlink, hyperlink text is the trailing test

on lines with no other content
----------

* Images
[urlforimage] show image at default size
[urlforimage width] show image on page at specified width reserving aspect ratio

* You Tube videos

[youtubeurl] to show video at default size
[youtubeurl width] to show video with secified width, preserving aspect ratio

<iframe width='560' height='315' src='//www.youtube.com/embed/McpQ3w0O14w?vq=hd720'
frameborder='0' allowfullscreen></iframe>

* Vimeo videos

[vimeourl] to show video at default size
[vimeourl width] to show video with specified width, preserving aspect ratio
